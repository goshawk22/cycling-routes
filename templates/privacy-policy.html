Privacy Policy

This website is dedicated to sharing user-submitted cycling routes. We respect your privacy and aim to collect as little information as necessary.

Data Collection

  • When users submit routes, we collect the route data itself (e.g., text, GPS coordinates, descriptions).
  • No personal identifying information is collected or required.

Cookies

We may use essential cookies to ensure the site works correctly. We do not use third-party tracking or analytics cookies.

Data Usage

  • Submitted routes may be displayed publicly on the website.
  • We do not share, sell, or otherwise distribute route data to third parties.

External Links

The site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at dev@goshawk22.uk.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the site constitutes your acceptance of any changes.
